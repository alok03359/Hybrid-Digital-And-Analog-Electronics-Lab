4 -To- 16 Decoder

Post Test

Q1.Using 3 to 8 line Decoder we can construct
  1. 4 to 16 decoder
  2. 5 to 32 decoder
  3. 2 to 4 decoder
  4. 3 to10 decoder

  (A) Only a
  (B) Both a & d
  (C) All
  (D) Only a & b
Ans is (D)


Q2.IC 74138 can be used for
  (A) 1 to 8 demultiplexer only
  (B) 3 to 8 decoder only
  (C) 3 to 10 decoder only
  (D) Both a. & b.
Ans is (D)


Q3.Number of 74138 ICs required to get 1 to 24 Decoder is
  (A) 3
  (B) 2
  (C) 4
  (D) 1
Ans is (A)


Q4.How many 3- to -8 line decoders are required for 1-to 13 -decoder?
  (A) 3
  (B) 2
  (C) 4
  (D) 1
Ans is (C)


Q5.Which gate is used with four 74138 IC to get 1 to 32 Decoder ?
  (A) AND gate
  (B) OR gate
  (C) XOR gate
  (D) NOT gate
Ans is (D)


Q6.A circuit which converts some binary code into a singular active output representing its numerical value is a
  (A) Demultiplexer
  (B) Comparator
  (C) Decoder
  (D) Multiplexer
Ans is (C)


Q7.The delay time of IC 74138 is _______ typical
  (A) access time memory
  (B) Comparable with
  (C) Higher than
  (D) Less than
Ans is (C)


Q8.Which of the following represents number of output lines for decoder with four input lines?
  (A) 14
  (B) 15
  (C) 16
  (D) 17
Ans is (C)
Q9. _______ are used for converting one type of number system into another type of number system.
  (A) Decoder
  (B) Logic gate
  (C) Half adder
  (D) Full adder
Ans is (A)

Q10.Which line goes low if the input to the 3- to 8 decoder (74LS 138) (A1- A0) = 1101111…

  (A) 1
  (B) 3
  (C) 5
  (D) 6
Ans is (B)
