Astable Multivibrator

Post Test

Q1. An Astable multivibrator has
  (A) one stable state
  (B) two stable states
  (C) no stable state
  (D) none of the above
Ans is (C)


Q2. The external triggering is not needed for the transition of state in the
  (A) Bistable multivibrator
  (B) Monostable multivibrator
  (C) Astable multivibrator
  (D) Both b and c
Ans is (C)


Q3.How to achieve 50% duty cycle in adjustable rectangular wave generator? (Assume R1 –>Resistor connected between supply and discharge and R2 –> Resistor connected between discharge and trigger input.)
  (A) R1   (B) R1>R2
  (C) R1=R2
  (D) R1 ≥ R2
Ans is (C)


Q4.When 555 timer is configured as an astable multivibrator, its duty cycle is determined by
  (A) internal resistors
  (B) external resistors
  (C) capacitors
  (D) inductors
Ans is (B)


Q5.What is the duty cycle of the waveform at the output of the circuit given below?

  (A) 78%
  (B) 56%
  (C) 50%
  (D) 44%
Ans is (B)


Q6.Find the charging and discharging time of 0.5µF capacitor.

  (A) Charging time=2ms; Discharging time=5ms
  (B) Charging time=2ms; Discharging time=5ms
  (C) Charging time=3ms; Discharging time=5ms
  (D) Charging time=5ms; Discharging time=3ms
Ans is (B)


Q7.Determine the output frequency of the circuit.

  (A) 1450Hz
  (B) 1333Hz
  (C) 1871Hz
  (D) 1700Hz
Ans is (C)


Q8.Astable multivibrator cannot be used for frequency division?
  (A) True
  (B) False
Ans is (B)
Q9.An Astable multivibrator operating at 150Hz has a discharge time of 2.5m. Find the duty cycle of the circuit.
  (A) 50%
  (B) 75%
  (C) 95.99%
  (D) 37.5%
Ans is (D)

Q10.An astable multivibrator circuit using IC 555 timer is shown below. Assume that the circuit is oscillating steadily

  (A) 3V to 5V
  (B) 3V to 6V
  (C) 3.6V to 6V
  (D) 3.6V to 5V
Ans is (B)
